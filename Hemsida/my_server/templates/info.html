Controls and info

Info

The game is about winning over your oppenent by shootig him or her with a projectile, and avoiding lurking monsters that are unable to be killed. By surviving and killing your oppenent a winning font will show up and a win will be added to your profile.

Player

The player is of a dark blue color. To move a character use: w for up, s for down, a for left and d for right. To shoot a projectile use the space-bar or left-click if you have a mouse. The projectiles direction is chosen by your last move by your character which is a part of the game difficulty. Each player has three or one heart dependent on the game map, the hearts are displayed in the right and left corners of the game map. Have fun!

Monster

The monsters are green and lurk around in the game, waiting to kill player characters by touch. They are also unkillble so stay warry!

Wall

The walls are light blue, and are unable to be destoryed and pose a great threat to player characters. Tight spaces may give monsters or opponents a advatage in game so choose a safe place to shoot projectiles from.

Build game

Build game

Info

If you navigate to play build game in the navbar you will be able to create your very own level. This is done by choosing settings, placing monsters, walls, choosing a title and a desctiption. When you click save game your map will be saved in profile and it will be shown globaly in list levels

Settings

As of right now there exists only one setting. You are able to choose either three or one heart for the players to begin with on the map

Place Monsters and Walls

You can place walls by left-clicking on the canvas grid, to remove a placed wall right-click instead. To place a monster click on the button monster which will be shown disabled. When disabled you will be able to place monsters the same way as you did with walls. Note that you can only remove walls when you have wall selected and monster when you have monster selected. You are also only able to place a max of six monster each level.

Tile and Description

you can choose a tilte with up to 15 characters and a Description with up to 150 chracters. Note that spaces are included, have fun!
